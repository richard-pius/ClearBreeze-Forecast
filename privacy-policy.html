ClearBreeze Forecast

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

Richard Pius ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") built ClearBreeze Forecast as a free, open-source application licensed under the MIT License. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use our Service.

By using ClearBreeze Forecast, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

📍 1. Information We Collect

ClearBreeze Forecast is designed with privacy as a core principle. We minimize data collection to only what is strictly necessary to deliver weather forecasts and air quality information.

Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location (precise and/or approximate) solely to determine your geographic coordinates for fetching locally relevant weather and air quality data. This location data is:

  • Used in real-time only to make API requests for your area
  • Never transmitted to any server owned or operated by us
  • Never stored on any remote server or database
  • Never shared with any third party for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be unambiguous about what we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage or behavioral analytics
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to our servers
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Account credentials (no login or account system exists)

⚙️ 2. How We Use Information

Your location data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Weather Forecasts: Your coordinates are sent to the MET Norway Weather API (api.met.no) to retrieve weather forecast data for your location.
  • Air Quality Data: Your coordinates are sent to the OpenAQ API (api.openaq.org) to retrieve air quality measurements near your location.
Your coordinates are transmitted directly from your device to these third-party APIs over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. The Developer does not operate any intermediary server and does not have access to, intercept, or log these requests.

💾 3. Data Storage

ClearBreeze Forecast caches weather and air quality data locally on your device using the platform's shared preferences storage mechanism. This cached data:

  • Remains entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any server
  • Is used to improve app performance and reduce redundant network requests
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data or cache through your device settings
  • Is automatically removed when you uninstall the app

We do not operate any backend servers, cloud databases, or remote storage systems. All data processing happens locally on your device.

🔗 4. Third-Party Services

The app communicates with the following third-party APIs to provide its core functionality. These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data received through API requests:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
MET Norway Weather API Weather forecast data met.no/privacy
OpenAQ API Air quality measurements openaq.org/privacy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services. We are not responsible for the data practices of these external providers. Note that when your device sends location coordinates to these APIs, the providers may process that data according to their own policies.

ClearBreeze Forecast does not integrate any advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics), social media plugins, or any other third-party tracking technologies.

🚫 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any user data with third parties. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to share.

The only data transmission that occurs is the sending of your device's coordinates directly to the MET Norway and OpenAQ APIs for the sole purpose of fetching weather and air quality information, as described above.

🛡️ 6. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar data protection laws worldwide, you have certain rights regarding your personal data. Because ClearBreeze Forecast does not collect, store, or process personal data on any server, most of these rights are inherently satisfied:

  • Right of Access & Portability: No personal data is stored on our systems, so there is no data to access or export.
  • Right to Erasure: You can clear all locally cached data by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can revoke location permission at any time through your device's settings to stop the app from accessing your location.
  • Right to Object: Since we do not process data for profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond providing weather information, this right is inherently respected.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6): When you grant location permission, the legal basis for processing your location data is your explicit consent. You may withdraw this consent at any time by disabling location permissions for the app in your device settings.

🔒 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure your data is handled securely:

  • All network communication with third-party APIs uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No personal data is transmitted to or stored on any server operated by us
  • Locally cached data is stored using the platform's standard secure storage mechanisms
  • The app's source code is publicly available for inspection under the MIT License

While no method of electronic transmission or local storage is 100% secure, the app's architecture — with no backend server and no data collection — minimizes potential security risks.

👦 8. Children's Privacy

ClearBreeze Forecast is suitable for users of all ages (13+). The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Since the app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations. No user accounts, chat features, or social interactions exist within the app.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information through this app, please contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.
